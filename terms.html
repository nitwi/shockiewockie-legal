ShockieWockie Terms of Service

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Service Description

ShockieWockie is a Discord bot used by the ShockieWockie crew to manage crew applications, member profiles, commitments, proof submissions, absences, reminders, leaderboards, moderation actions, and related officer workflows.

2. Eligibility and Access

Use of the bot is limited to Discord servers where ShockieWockie has been added and configured by server administrators. Some commands and controls are restricted to configured administrators, officers, or crew members.

3. Acceptable Use

You agree not to abuse, spam, exploit, or intentionally disrupt the bot. You also agree not to submit false, misleading, stolen, reused, or manipulated proof or application information.

4. Crew Management Decisions

Server administrators and configured officers are responsible for reviewing applications, proof submissions, absence requests, commitment records, strikes, demotions, corrections, and other crew-management decisions. The bot provides tracking and workflow tools but does not independently decide crew membership.

5. Availability and Changes

The bot may be updated, interrupted, limited, or removed at any time. Features, commands, storage behavior, and review workflows may change as the bot is maintained.

6. No Warranty

The bot is provided as-is without warranties of availability, accuracy, or fitness for a particular purpose. Server staff should review important bot output before relying on it for crew decisions.

For questions about these Terms, contact the bot operator on Discord at nitw_t or on GitHub at nitwi.
